# Solana UI/UX Onboarding Kit

Final synthesis of the heuristic analysis carried out by the researchers.

The Solana ecosystem was built by and for those who already master its rules. For someone arriving from Web2, onboarding is the border between adopting and abandoning, and — in a context of irreversible actions — between protecting oneself and losing funds. This document maps where that onboarding fails the beginner and shows that the failures repeat enough to become patterns, natural candidates for an entry-point design system.

{% hint style="info" %}
**By the numbers:** 13 apps evaluated · 30 items per app · **390 items** total · Nielsen protocol (10 heuristics × 3 questions) · averages from **0.53** (Tangem, the benchmark) to **4.00** (Orca and Kamino, total absence of an entry layer). A snapshot of the market in the **first half of 2026**.
{% endhint %}

**The thesis, in one sentence.** Solana's onboarding failures are not idiosyncratic — they are patterns. And recurring patterns call for systemic solutions: an entry-point design system, open source and easy to adopt, not one-off app-by-app fixes.
